Experience

The art of doing very little

We do not programme your day. We provide the setting — a garden, a veranda, a cool room, a cold drink — and let Alghero do the rest.

7:00 — 10:00

Breakfast served
on the veranda

Our breakfast is uncomplicated and generous. Fresh bread from the forno across the street. Local pecorino and cured ham. Seasonal fruit — figs in August, blood oranges in winter. Pastries that do not last long. Proper coffee, made to order.

In warm weather, we set the long table on the outdoor veranda, under the shade of a canvas awning. The garden is just beyond. Guests tend to linger here, reading the paper or planning their day over a second cup.

Choose from buffet, continental, or a lighter Italian-style breakfast. Dietary requirements are happily accommodated — just let us know when you book.

Local & Fresh

Bread from the neighbourhood bakery. Cheese from the hills. Fruit from the market. We keep it close to home.

Made to Order

Espresso, cappuccino, or a long American. Our bar opens at seven, and the coffee is always fresh.

Eat Outside

The veranda catches the morning breeze. On warm days, there is no better place to start the morning.

All Day

A garden for
reading, napping,
or doing nothing at all

Our garden is not manicured. It is kept — loosely, naturally. Mature pines provide shade. Jasmine climbs one wall. Bougainvillea spills over another. The gravel paths crunch pleasantly underfoot.

There are chairs and small tables scattered throughout, never all occupied at once. Guests read here. They nap. They open a bottle of wine from the bar and watch the light change. It is the kind of garden that rewards slowness.

In the evening, the garden lamps come on and the atmosphere shifts. Quiet conversation. The smell of jasmine. A drink before bed.

Atmosphere

The details that matter

The Billiard Room

A relic from another era, and we mean that kindly. The table is old but level. The cues are worn smooth. It is a room for slow games in the evening, for conversation between shots, for passing time without checking a phone. One of those details that makes a small hotel memorable.

The Bar

Open from seven in the morning until half past ten at night. Coffee to start the day. Cold beer after the beach. Aperitivo at sunset — we keep a small selection of local vermouth and Sardinian wines. The bartender knows most guests by name by the second day.

Free Parking

Our private car park is video-monitored and included with every stay. No reservations needed — just pull in.

Bike Rental

Rent a bicycle and follow the seaside promenade into town. The flat coastal path is perfect for a lazy afternoon ride.

Concierge

We are here from 6:30am to 10:30pm with local recommendations, restaurant bookings, and directions. Your magnetic key gives you 24-hour access.

Alghero

A town that moves
to its own rhythm

Alghero is not a place for rushing. The old town is a maze of cobblestoned lanes that open unexpectedly onto the sea. The harbour fills with fishing boats in the late afternoon. The restaurants along the bastions serve fregola with clams and local vermentino until midnight.

From Hotel Mistral, you can walk the pine-shaded promenade into town in about thirty minutes. Or take the bus that stops right outside. The beach is closer — five minutes on foot, and you are on the sand at Maria Pia.

We are happy to point you toward our favourite spots: the bakeries, the quiet coves, the restaurants where the owners still cook.
