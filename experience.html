The Experience

The art of doing very little.

A stay at Hotel Mistral is deliberately simple. We provide the setting—a quiet garden, a comfortable bed, a generous breakfast—and you set the pace.

— 01 / Morning

Breakfast
in the veranda.

Our breakfast is served in the bright, glass-enclosed veranda overlooking the garden. We believe a good day starts with good food, prepared simply.

The buffet features fresh pastries from the local bakery, traditional Sardinian sweets, cold cuts, cheeses, yogurt, and fresh fruit. The coffee is real Italian espresso, made to order at the bar.

— 02 / Afternoon & Evening

A drink in
the shade.

The hotel bar is open from 7 AM until 11 PM. It's a quiet place to retreat from the midday sun, read a book, or enjoy an aperitivo before heading into town for dinner.

Take your drink to the garden terrace, or if the evening is cool, retreat to the lounge where our billiard table is always available for guests.
