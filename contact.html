At a Glance

Everything you need to know

Check-in & Check-out

Check-in is from 2:00pm. If you arrive earlier, you are welcome to leave your luggage and use the garden. Check-out is by 10:00am. Late check-out may be available on request.

Parking

Free private parking is available on site, monitored by video. Spaces are not assigned — simply park where you find room. No reservation is necessary.

Pets

Please contact us in advance if you plan to travel with a pet. We do our best to accommodate, but restrictions may apply depending on the room type.

Children

Children of all ages are welcome. Cots are available on request, subject to availability. The garden and nearby beach make this a relaxed choice for families.

Payment

We accept all major credit cards. For bookings made directly, a deposit may be required. For online bookings, standard Booking.com terms apply.

Cancellation

Cancellation policies vary by rate and season. Please refer to your booking confirmation for specific terms, or contact us directly with any questions.
