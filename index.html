The Hotel

A family-run hotel,
simply done well.

Hotel Mistral sits on the quiet edge of Alghero, a short walk from Maria Pia Beach and the pine-scented promenade that leads into the old town. For over three decades, our family has welcomed guests into 25 rooms spread across three floors, each one kept clean, cool, and comfortable.

We are not a resort. We are a place to sleep well, eat a good breakfast, and step outside into the unhurried rhythm of Sardinia. The bar opens at seven. The garden is always there. And the beach is five minutes away on foot.

Experience

Breakfast on the veranda,
then the day is yours.

Mornings here begin with the smell of fresh bread and coffee drifting from the veranda. We serve a simple buffet with local cheeses, cured meats, seasonal fruit, and pastries from the bakery down the road. Eat outside if the weather allows — the garden is at its best before ten.

After breakfast, walk to Maria Pia Beach through the pinewood. Rent a bicycle and follow the coast. Or simply stay put: the billiard room is open, the bar serves cold drinks until late evening, and the garden chairs are never all taken.

Explore the Experience

The Garden

Shaded by mature trees and bordered by jasmine, our garden is where guests read, nap, or simply sit with a drink from the bar.

The Billiard Room

A quiet corner for a slow game in the evening. One of those small details that makes a hotel feel like a home.

The Bar

Open from 7am to 10:30pm. Coffee in the morning, aperitivo at sunset, and quiet conversation after dinner.

Amenities

Everything you need,
nothing you do not.

We believe in thoughtful simplicity. Every room has air conditioning, free WiFi, a safe, and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. Downstairs, you will find parking, a garden, and a bar that stays open late.
