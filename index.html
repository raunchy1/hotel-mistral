— 01 / The Hotel

A family-run hotel,
simply done well.

Hotel Mistral sits on the quiet edge of Alghero, a short walk from Maria Pia Beach and the pine-scented promenade that leads into the old town. For over three decades, our family has welcomed guests into 25 rooms spread across three floors, each one kept clean, cool, and comfortable.

We are not a resort. We are a place to sleep well, eat a good breakfast, and step outside into the unhurried rhythm of Sardinia. The bar opens at seven. The garden is always there. And the beach is five minutes away on foot.

Slow mornings,

Breakfast is served in the veranda, filled with natural light and fresh local products. Fresh bread from the bakery downstairs, local cheeses, seasonal fruits, and real Italian coffee.

From there, the day is yours. The beach is a ten-minute walk away, the historical center is eight minutes by car, or you can simply enjoy the garden and a chair in the shade.
