Location

Close to everything.
Hidden from crowds.

We are tucked into a quiet residential pocket of Alghero — close enough to Maria Pia Beach for a morning swim, far enough from the tourist bustle to actually hear yourself think. The old town is eight minutes by car. The airport is twelve. Perfect location is not about being in the middle. It is about being exactly where you want to be.

  • 300m Maria Pia Beach 10 min walk
  • 2.5km Historic Center 8 min by car
  • 6km Alghero Airport 12 min by car
  • 15km Neptune's Grotto 25 min by car
