What is around us

Maria Pia Beach

Just five minutes on foot. Maria Pia is one of the most beautiful beaches in Alghero — a long stretch of fine sand backed by a fragrant pine forest. The water is shallow and clear, perfect for swimming from May through October.

Distance: 300 metres · Walk: 5 minutes

Alghero Old Town (Centro Storico)

A walled Catalan city perched on the sea. The old town is a maze of narrow lanes, coral workshops, and piazzas that open onto the water. Walk the bastions at sunset, then dine on fresh seafood at one of the harbour restaurants.

Distance: 2.4 km · Walk: 30 minutes along the seafront · Bus: Direct from hotel

Porto Conte & Capo Caccia

A short drive north takes you to the Porto Conte Natural Reserve and the dramatic cliffs of Capo Caccia. Neptune's Grotto — a vast sea cave accessible by boat or by the Escala del Cabirol stairway — is worth the trip.

Distance: 21 km · Drive: 30 minutes

Nuraghe di Palmavera

One of the best-preserved nuragic complexes in Sardinia, dating back over 3,000 years. A fascinating glimpse into the island's pre-Roman history, set in a quiet countryside location just outside town.

Distance: 7.2 km · Drive: 15 minutes

Getting Here

How to reach us

directions_car

By Car

From Alghero-Fertilia Airport, follow the SS127bis south for 6 km. Turn left onto Via Liguria. We have free private parking on site — no reservation needed.

directions_bus

By Bus

A bus stop is located directly outside the hotel. Buses run regularly to the old town, the airport, and nearby beaches. Tickets available at reception.

train

By Train

Alghero railway station is a 12-minute walk from the hotel. Direct trains connect to Sassari and other Sardinian towns. We can arrange a taxi from the station if needed.

flight

By Plane

Alghero-Fertilia Airport (AHO) is 6 km away. Direct flights from Rome, Milan, London, and other European cities. A taxi takes about 15 minutes; the bus stops right outside our door.
